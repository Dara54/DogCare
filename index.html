Dogs in our shelter


Our shelter now houses 6 beautiful dogs. Bella, a sweet and gentle Labrador retriever, was found wandering the streets, lost and alone. Max, the playful Border Collie mix, was surrendered by its previous owners, who could no longer take care of it. Luna, a tiny black Maltipoo, that is known for its hypoallergenic coat, was rescued from the rubble after a fire. Rocky is a Beagle that was found in a cardboard box near a dumpster. Daisy, a furry Corgi who was sadly abandoned by its family when they moved to another town. Oliver is a German shepherd who used to work with police and helped to find drugs.
With care, and the support of the shelter volunteers, Bella, Max, Luna, Rocky, Daisy, and Oliver blossomed into happy, healthy dogs.

Our Additional Services

At Dog care, we go beyond providing a safe haven for animals in need. We offer a range of additional services to ensure the well-being and happiness of our furry residents.


Grooming Services:

Our professional grooming services are tailored to keep your pets looking and feeling their best. From regular haircuts to nail trims, our experienced groomers provide top-notch care to enhance the overall health and appearance of your beloved companions.

Bathing Sessions:

Treat your pets to a spa-like experience with our bathing sessions. We use pet-friendly products to cleanse and pamper your furry friends, leaving them refreshed and smelling delightful. Bathing is not only about cleanliness but also contributes to the health of their skin and coat.

Dog Walking:

Our dedicated staff members love spending quality time outdoors with our canine residents. Dog walking is not just exercise; it's an opportunity for socialization, mental stimulation, and the sheer joy of exploration. Our scheduled walks ensure that each dog receives the attention and exercise they need.

Playgroups and Socialization:

We organize playgroups to encourage social interaction among our animals. This helps them develop positive behavior, build friendships, and enjoy a sense of community. Our staff carefully monitors these interactions to ensure a safe and enjoyable environment.

Training:

Join our training workshops to strengthen the bond between you and your pet. From basic commands to behavioral training, our experts provide valuable insights and techniques to foster a harmonious relationship with your furry companions.

At Dog care, we believe in comprehensive care for our animals, and these additional services reflect our commitment to their physical and emotional well-being.
